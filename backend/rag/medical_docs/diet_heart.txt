HEART-HEALTHY DIET GUIDELINES

This document provides educational dietary information for heart health.

IMPORTANCE OF A HEART-HEALTHY DIET

A balanced diet helps:
- Control cholesterol levels
- Maintain healthy blood pressure
- Reduce risk of heart disease
- Support overall cardiovascular function

RECOMMENDED FOODS

1. Fruits and Vegetables
- Apples, oranges, berries
- Spinach, broccoli, carrots
- Rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber

2. Whole Grains
- Brown rice
- Whole wheat bread
- Oats
- Barley

3. Healthy Fats
- Olive oil
- Nuts and seeds
- Avocados
- Fatty fish (salmon, tuna)

4. Lean Protein
- Fish
- Skinless poultry
- Beans and lentils
- Low-fat dairy products

FOODS TO LIMIT OR AVOID

- Processed foods
- Excess salt
- Sugary drinks
- Fried foods
- Red and processed meats
- Trans fats and saturated fats

DIETARY TIPS

- Reduce sodium intake to control blood pressure
- Eat smaller, frequent meals
- Read food labels carefully
- Maintain portion control

Healthy eating combined with exercise significantly reduces heart disease risk.
