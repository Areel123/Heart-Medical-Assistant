COMMON HEART CHECKUP TESTS

This document provides general information about routine heart health tests.

BASIC HEART CHECKUP TESTS

1. Blood Pressure Measurement
Used to assess hypertension or hypotension.

2. Blood Tests
Includes:
- Cholesterol levels
- Blood sugar levels
- Cardiac enzymes (if heart attack suspected)

3. Electrocardiogram (ECG or EKG)
Records electrical activity of the heart.
Helps detect abnormal heart rhythms and past heart attacks.

4. Echocardiogram
Ultrasound test that shows heart structure and function.

WHEN TESTS ARE RECOMMENDED

Heart tests may be recommended if:
- Symptoms like chest pain or shortness of breath appear
- There is a family history of heart disease
- Blood pressure or cholesterol is high

Regular checkups help detect heart problems early and improve long-term outcomes.
